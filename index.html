History of Cookies
The first cookies originated from the 7th Century A.D. They were hypothesised to be test cakes that bakers use to test the oven temperature. War and exploration eventually brought sugar to the Mediterranean area and European countries. After the Industrial Revolution, improvements in technology led to more variety of cookies be available commercially, such as butter and chocolate cookies, amongst many others.
